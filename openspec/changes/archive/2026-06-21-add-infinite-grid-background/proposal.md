## Why

Replicating the signature "infinite grid" background of the Pascube benchmark application inside PascubeDB aligns the visual branding and aesthetics of the database dashboard with the core benchmark app, providing a cohesive, retro-futuristic, and high-fidelity user experience.

## What Changes

- Add a subtle, animated 3D perspective grid receding to a horizon in the background of the dashboard.
- The grid lines will glow with a low-opacity primary indigo/cyan gradient that blends seamlessly with the existing dark glassmorphism theme and glowing radial backdrops.
- Incorporate a smooth scrolling animation to give a sense of forward movement, matching the benchmark application.
- Apply linear/radial mask fades near the top and edges to smoothly blend the grid into the dark background space without hard cuts.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities

- `dashboard-infinite-grid`: Add a premium, animated 3D perspective infinite grid background to the dashboard.
